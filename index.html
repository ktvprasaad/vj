About Me

I'm an accomplished software professional committed to contributing great solutions in information technology for over 14+ years.

Having achieved with development, maintenance, and support projects in the Waterfall software development model for over a decade in Mainframe technology, I am maturing Agile now.

I have been in different business domains and geographical locations. Being with varied team sizes, I have learned a lot and saved thousands of dollars for the clients. As I foresee the need for classic technology is getting lesser than modern technology, I'm upskilling my coding skills to Web applications. In fact, some of the skillsets that I have supported had already been sunset by the vendors!

It took me a while to upskill my competency from legacy to modern technology. After pondering over various technologies, I determined to pursue web development! It is my instinct that drove me to stride into the UI development path. Currently, I am designing and developing the Front-end components for one of the startups of my buddy!

As a continuous learner who has upskilled accelerated web development training, I am eager to be part of a team where I can offer my IT experience and create efficient software products through my problem solving skills and creative quality solutions. I am hoping to work with you soon!

About Me

I React, yet Responsible! After 14+ years of experience in Mainframe Technology, today my passion is to be more of an UI professional.

I have always been committed in my roles and responsibilities which I have worked and have been working so far. Being there and having done with maintenance and support activities in a Water Fall software development model for over a decade, I’m turning into more Agile with the changing times.

Though I have been in different business domains and geographical locations with varied team sizes, I feel I have not done any phenomenal stuff or saved billions of dollars in the technology realm so far, probably I have been with the classical applications which were already stabilized and no much room to progress in my perspective. In fact, some of the technology skillsets which I have supported, had already sunset by the vendors themselves!

It took me a while to upskill my competency from legacy to modern technology. Probably I’m a Turtle rather than a Hare! I think it is better to be late than never. It required me some confidence to take this decision and mindset to compete with the already experienced young professionals. It is my instinct rather than the statistical data of my work experience that urges me to pursue in the UI path with robustness. Currently I'm designing and developing front-end components for one of the startups of my buddy.

As a continuous learner, I’m eager to be part of your team where I can offer my IT experience, take challenges and bring success. Hoping to work with you soon!
